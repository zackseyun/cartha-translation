# Nehemiah — book context for review

- **Source**: Westminster Leningrad Codex (WLC) / Unicode Hebrew Bible (UHB). 13 chapters. In the Hebrew Bible, Ezra-Nehemiah is one book.
- **Form**: Memoir sections (the "Nehemiah Memoir") interspersed with lists, prayers, and narrative. First-person throughout much of the book.
- **Date**: Events ca. 445–430 BC; final editing post-exilic.
- **Original audience**: The Jerusalem community during reconstruction — identity, security, covenant renewal.

## Key translation issues

1. **The Nehemiah Memoir (1st person)**: Chapters 1–7 and 12–13 are in Nehemiah's first person. Preserve this — do not shift to third person.
2. **Nehemiah's prayers**: Short, direct prayers embedded in the narrative (1:5–11; 5:19; 6:9, 14; 13:14, 22, 29, 31). These are personal, not liturgical — keep their directness.
3. **The great covenant renewal** (ch. 8–10): The public reading of the Torah by Ezra, the people's response, and the covenant signing. The liturgical solemnity must be preserved. "The Law of Moses" — keep "Law" (not "instruction" — though that is literally tôrāh, "Law" is the established English rendering in legal contexts).
4. **Lists** (chs. 7; 10; 11–12): Comprehensive lists of returnees, signatories, priests, Levites. Render all proper names accurately — these lists are part of Israel's identity documentation.
5. **"Remember me, O my God"** (5:19; 13:14, 22, 31): Nehemiah's characteristic prayer. Keep "remember" — the same root as in Psalm 25, 89, etc. A covenant appeal.
6. **Mixed marriages controversy** (13:23–27): Nehemiah's confrontation over intermarriage — physically forceful (13:25: "I… pulled out their hair"). Render the physicality accurately.

## Style / register

- Personal memoir style: direct, practical, sometimes agitated.
- The covenant renewal (ch. 8–10) is solemn and communal — register shift from the memoir.

## Known pitfalls

- **Softening Nehemiah's physical actions** (pulling out hair, 13:25) — render accurately.
- **Varying "remember me, O God"** — it is a recurring formula.
