captive in prison. There is, of course, another alternative, which is only conjectural, and yet is not altogether impossible,
that the line κατακαμπτόμενος . . . σιδηρῳ has been inserted to identify the utterer of the Prayer with Manasses ; and that
the following line, εἰς τὸ . . . ἄνεσις, is a further expansion of the gloss. On the other hand, if κατακαμπτόμενος κτλ. is
