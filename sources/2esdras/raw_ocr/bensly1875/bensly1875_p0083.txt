105. omnes enim portabunt...] The Arab. has
جميعهم يحمل كل احد
Ockley: But every one shall stand for all (that he
hath done). Steiner would alter the last word to
ܢܩܘܡ wonder jeder Einzelne steht ein für sein
Thun. I prefer to read ܢܩܘܡ ‘each shall stand
with his burden,’ this would involve less change,
and at the same time preserve an idea which is
expressed in the oldest versions.
